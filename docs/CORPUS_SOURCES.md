# Legally Legal Corpus Sources

This document lists the primary sources used to build the hackathon MVP corpus in `backend/src/main/resources/corpus/corpus.json`.

## Federal

| Instrument | Source type |
|------------|-------------|
| Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria 1999 (as amended) | Official consolidated text — fundamental rights (ss. 35, 36, 43, 44) |
| Police Act 2020 | Federal legislation — duties, search, seizure |
| Administration of Criminal Justice Act 2015 | Federal legislation — arrest, digital evidence practice |
| Cybercrimes (Prohibition, Prevention, Etc.) Act 2015 | Federal legislation — communications privacy |
| Land Use Act 1978 | Federal legislation — land vesting and alienation consent |
| Evidence Act 2011 | Federal legislation — documentary evidence |
| Legal Aid Act 2011 | Federal legislation — legal aid access |
| National Human Rights Commission Act | Federal legislation — complaint functions |
